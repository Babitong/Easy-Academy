About This Course

This comprehensive course covers all the fundamental mathematics concepts you need to build a strong foundation. Designed for beginners, it takes you from basic arithmetic operations through to introductory algebra concepts.

What You'll Learn

  • Perform all basic arithmetic operations with confidence
  • Understand fractions, decimals, and percentages
  • Solve basic algebraic equations
  • Apply mathematical concepts to real-world problems
  • Develop problem-solving and logical thinking skills

Requirements

  • No prior math knowledge required
  • Basic calculator (optional)
  • Notebook for practice problems

Course Curriculum

  • Understanding Whole Numbers 32 min
  • Place Value and Number Systems 45 min
  • Comparing and Ordering Numbers 28 min
  • Practice Exercises 30 min
  • Addition and Subtraction 40 min
  • Multiplication Techniques 35 min
  • Division Methods 38 min
  • Order of Operations 25 min
  • Word Problems 45 min
